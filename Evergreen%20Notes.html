Evergreen Notes

jakob11th May 2020 at 9:51am

Most notes are transient. But such notes don't help to produce novel and better insights.

In contrast, evergreen notes represent a linked repertoire of insights that gain in value over time as it becomes possible to discover novel connections. This is possible through the following properties of evergreen notes:

  • Evergreen notes are atomic
  • Evergreen notes are concept-oriented
  • Evergreen notes are densely linked
  • Evergreen note titles are like APIs
  • Evergreen note titles should contain strong claims

Since each evergreen note represents an insight, the number of evergreen notes written per day are a way to measure the Productivity of knowledge workers.

A notebook full of evergreen notes is an external thinking device full of signal and no noise.
