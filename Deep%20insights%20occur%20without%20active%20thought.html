Deep insights occur without active thought

jakob11th May 2020 at 8:00am

Novel insights occur in a three step process:

  1. a stage of preparation in which the problem is consciously worked over
  2. a period of incubation without any conscious concentration upon the problem
  3. and then the illumination which is later justified by logic.
