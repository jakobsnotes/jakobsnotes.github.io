Writing aids thinking and understanding

jakob11th May 2020 at 4:51pm

As long as ideas only exist in your head they appear better than they really are. Writing puts pressure on your thinking and reveals all the nuances you don't sharply understand.

Writing is powerful because The best way to learn is to teach. By writing you're teaching to an imaginary audience.

Moreover, by writing you can free up mental space. (People struggle to absorb new material when their working memory is already overloaded.)

A good way to put it is that writing allows us to create a surface to think on.
